MileSplit Live - Redesign

MileSplit publishes data and editorial content centered around track & field and cross country. MileSplit Live, a complementary product to MileSplit, enables people to stay up-to-date with track and cross country meets in real-time, and is used by countless athletes, coaches, and parents each season.

Although MileSplit Live was successful, it had been scarcely updated since it’s original launch. Recognizing the product’s potential for improvement, I was brought on to improve its aesthetics, usability, and financial performance.

Understanding the Problem

I began the project by talking to current customers about how they were using the product, what they liked about it, and what they didn’t. I learned that it was meeting their basic needs, but was lacking depth. These conversations left me with actionable objectives to pursue when I began designs.

It’s cool to see that an athlete ranked in the top ten of the state, but I want to know more...
Yeah, I want those details... if you're a top 3 runner out of lane 8, that says something.
I show past performances to my daughter, so she can see how athletes progress throughout the season.

Energizing The Brand

The product had a design system that was functional, but uninspiring. I enhanced and expanded the system to better fit the energy of track & field.

Final Designs

Improving Navigability

MileSplit's database was a treasure trove of valuable data and content that we could integrate into the product. However, no matter how rich the data, it would be of little use if customers couldn’t find it.

To encourage discoverability of data and content, we improved the event list and filters, and added new functionality, such as search.

Showcasing Rankings and Records

Rankings and records play a significant role in track and cross country. They serve as a means for competitors and fans to stay up-to-date with the sport.

To fulfill this interest, we found new and captivating ways to showcase rankings and records information in the product.

Optimizing Scan-ability

Event results are the bread and butter of MileSplit Live, so it was critical to get these pages right. They were doing a decent job, but needed some improvements. So, I redesigned the tables to be more visually appealing and easier to scan.

Integrating Athlete Profiles

Athletes are the heart of the sport, and behind each record and finishing time is a story. To provide a more comprehensive experience, we introduced athlete profiles to the product, enabling customers to delve into these stories.
