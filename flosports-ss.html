FloSports - Schedules & Scores

FloSports offers coverage and live streaming for a wide range of sports, from wrestling to racing. The service is accessible via various platforms including iOS and Android and features a schedule that allows customers to stay informed about upcoming events, games, and matches for their favorite sports.

The schedule feature was sufficient in the early days of the business, but FloSports was introducing thousands of new events to their offering and had plans of eventually providing additional features such as live scores.

I was tasked with redesigning the schedule to better support the influx of new events and lay the groundwork for future improvements.

Exploring the Possibilities

I began the project by conducting a high-level exploration of potential solutions. Following this exploration, I vetted the ideas with stakeholders and converged on a direction that we felt was promising.

Validating the Designs

After settling on a direction, I proceeded to create a prototype and user-test the designs. The testing went great and we were able to catch and address some issues before moving to the development stage.

Final Designs

Browsing by Date

We introduced features that let customers seamlessly navigate through time, whether they’re interested in what’s happening tomorrow, or what’s happening next month.

Event List

I designed the list components to prioritize critical event information while also ensuring they were uncluttered and easy to scan.

Filtering and Searching

We implemented filters and a search feature in order to help customers cut through the noise and get to the specific event or subset of events they’re interested in.

Future Concepts

Integrating scores into the schedule was not within the scope of the first phase of the project. However, we knew this was something our customers would love, and that we eventually wanted to do. So, I did some exploration to envision what that integration might look like.

Results

We saw great feedback from the user testing sessions, followed by positive increases in a few key metrics after launching. More importantly, we laid the groundwork for future, high-impact improvements.
